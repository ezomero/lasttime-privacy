Privacy Policy

LastTime - Recurring Task Tracker

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

🔒 Your Privacy Matters: LastTime is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your task data is yours and yours alone.

1. Overview

LastTime ("we", "our", or "the app") is a recurring task tracking application that helps you never forget important tasks. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

What We DON'T Collect:

  • ✅ We do NOT collect any personal information
  • ✅ We do NOT track your location
  • ✅ We do NOT collect your task data
  • ✅ We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
  • ✅ We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • ✅ We do NOT use analytics or crash reporting services

What We DO Collect:

NOTHING. All your data stays on your device.

3. How Your Data is Stored

All task data (titles, descriptions, dates, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device using secure device storage (AsyncStorage). Your data:

  • 📱 Stays on your phone/tablet
  • 🔒 Is NOT uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • 🚫 Is NOT synced to the cloud
  • 💾 Is only accessible by you on your device

💡 Important: If you uninstall the app or delete app data, all your tasks will be permanently deleted. We cannot recover them because we don't have copies.

4. Permissions

LastTime only requests permissions that are essential for core functionality:

Notifications

  • Why: To remind you about upcoming tasks
  • How: Scheduled locally on your device
  • Data: No notification data is sent to external servers

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

LastTime uses minimal third-party services:

Expo (React Native Framework)

  • Purpose: App development framework
  • Data: No personal data is shared with Expo
  • Privacy Policy: expo.dev/privacy

Apple Push Notification Service (iOS)

  • Purpose: Deliver notifications on iOS devices
  • Data: Device token only (no personal data)
  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

Google Cloud Messaging (Android)

  • Purpose: Deliver notifications on Android devices
  • Data: Device token only (no personal data)
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

LastTime does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages as it does not collect personal information.

7. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • ✅ Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • ✅ Edit: You can edit any task at any time
  • ✅ Delete: You can delete individual tasks or all data
  • ✅ Export: Currently not supported (all data is local)

8. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • 🔒 Data is stored using device-level encryption (iOS/Android standard)
  • 🚫 No data transmission over the internet
  • ✅ No server-side vulnerabilities (because there's no server)

9. International Users

LastTime is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no cross-border data transfers or data residency issues.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Notifying users in the app (for significant changes)

11. Open Source

LastTime values transparency. If you want to verify our privacy claims, you can:

  • Review the app's code (contact us for details)
  • Inspect network traffic (you'll see no data is sent)
  • Check device storage (data is local only)
